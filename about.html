Our Story

Crafting memories through scent, one bottle at a time.

The Halwa Philosophy

At Halwa, we believe that perfume is more than just a fragrance; it's an expression of identity and a vessel for memory. Our journey began with a simple passion for raw, natural ingredients and a desire to bring back the artistry of traditional perfumery.

We source our ingredients from sustainable farms around the world, ensuring that every note in our compositions is as authentic as it is beautiful.

Ethical Sourcing

We partner directly with growers to ensure fair wages and sustainable farming practices.

Small Batch

Every bottle is filled by hand in small batches to maintain the highest quality and freshness.

Cruelty Free

We never test on animals, and our products are free from animal-derived ingredients.
